## Get Started in 2 Minutes

Create your Assignix Grow account and start building your career journey in just a few steps.

<Steps>

<Step title="Create your account">

Go to **Assignix Grow** and click **Get Started**.

You can instantly sign up using **Google Authentication (OAuth)** or continue with your **email address**.

This allows you to quickly connect your account and securely access the platform.

</Step>

<Step title="Verify your email">

If you sign up using email, Assignix Grow will send a **One-Time Password (OTP)** to your inbox.

Enter the verification code to confirm your identity and activate your account securely.

Existing users can also log in using **OTP or their password**.

</Step>

<Step title="Choose your username">

After verification, new users will create a **unique username**.

This username becomes part of your **Assignix Grow profile URL**, allowing you to build and share your professional portfolio.

</Step>

<Step title="Complete onboarding">

Next, you’ll enter the **Assignix Grow onboarding flow** where you set your:

- Career interests  
- Preferred job domains  
- Skill level  
- Career goals  

These preferences allow Assignix AI to generate **personalized job recommendations and learning roadmaps**.

</Step>

<Step title="Start using Assignix Grow">

Once onboarding is complete, you will land on the **Assignix Grow dashboard**.

From here you can:

- Explore the **AI Learning Companion**
- Build your **career roadmap**
- Discover **job opportunities**
- Create **professional resumes**

Your career growth journey begins immediately.

</Step>

</Steps>
